# Contributors

CoronaPoker is developed by [@tonikelope](https://github.com/tonikelope).

## Bug reporters and testers

A heartfelt thank-you to the players whose persistent, methodical testing
and detailed bug reports keep this project moving forward. The hardest
bugs are the ones that hide behind specific timing, network conditions
and recovery edge cases, and they only surface when someone takes the
time to reproduce them and to send back the full picture.

### [@yxmgl](https://github.com/yxmgl)

Outstanding tester. Their systematic stress-testing of corner-case
scenarios (abrupt process kills mid-hand, network disconnections,
mid-game joiners, repeated recovery flows) and the quality of the debug
logs attached to every report were essential to pinpoint root causes that
would otherwise have been extremely hard to reproduce. 
Thanks for caring about the project at this level.
